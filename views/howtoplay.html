How to play?

Register with a unique username.

If a unique username is created successfully, you will be directed to login page.

Login with your username.

Once the game is launched, pick a guess from the scrollable word list.

Enter your guess and hit "Try".

Guess history will show the number of common letters between your guess and the secret word regardless of case and position.

If you are lucky to enter the same word as the secret word, congratulations, you win!
